ingredients:
  - "250ml plant-based milk (coconut, almond or oat)"
  - "1 teaspoon turmeric powder"
  - "1/4 teaspoon ginger powder (or freshly grated)"
  - "1 generous pinch of ground black pepper"
  - "1 pinch of cinnamon"
  - "1 teaspoon coconut oil or ghee (essential for absorption)"
  - "Optional: Natural sweetener to taste (stevia, raw honey)"
instructions:
  - "In a small saucepan, pour the plant-based milk and add all the spices: turmeric, ginger, pepper, cinnamon."
  - "Turn on medium heat and bring to a gentle boil, stirring with a whisk."
  - "Lower the heat and let it simmer gently for 2-3 minutes to activate the spices."
  - "Turn off the heat and add the coconut oil or ghee. Stir until melted."
  - "Pour into a mug, sweeten if desired, and drink hot."

Golden Milk isn't just a trendy drink—it's an ancient Ayurvedic remedy ("Haldi Doodh"). It's the perfect alternative to herbal tea or hot chocolate in the evening.

The chemical secret of this recipe is **synergy**:
1. **Turmeric** reduces inflammation.
2. **Black Pepper** (piperine) increases turmeric absorption by 2000%.
3. **Fats** (coconut oil/milk) carry curcumin, which is fat-soluble.

Drink it 30-60 minutes before bed to lower cortisol levels and prepare your body for deep rest.
